Our Services

Our unique teaching tools and methodologies allow teachers to demonstrate typical concepts of Science, Maths and Social Science by various experiments during their regular classroom sessions. These teaching aids are mapped with all curriculums like CBSE, ICSE, Cambridge, IB and State boards.

  • 5D approach of learning
  • Quarterly stress free assessment tools and feedbacks
  • Mentorship by current IIT students
  • Customized Teacher Training
  • Available for grade I to X
